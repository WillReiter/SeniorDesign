About our project

Motion Prediction using Artificial Intelligence has come quite a long way. Fifteen years ago, image recognition was one of the challenging AI/Machine Learning problems. In 2021, we have the computational power & technological requirements necessary to analize complex data sets. At its core, this project is the mission to fully utilize the state-of-the-art tools we have at our hands.
